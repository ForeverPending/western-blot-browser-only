Accessibility Statement

Last updated: July 5, 2026

We want the Western Blot Fold Change Analyzer to be usable by as many people as possible and aim to conform to the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 at the AA level.

What we’ve done

  • Semantic HTML structure and labeled form controls.
  • Color contrast intended to meet WCAG AA in both light and dark themes.
  • Keyboard-operable controls and visible focus indication.
  • A light/dark theme and respect for reduced-motion preferences.
  • Data tables that present the same numbers shown in charts.

Known limitations

Some visualizations are drawn on an HTML canvas, which is inherently harder for assistive technology to read. Where a chart is shown, the underlying values are also available as a table and as CSV/XLSX export so the same information can be reached without the visual.

Feedback

If you encounter an accessibility barrier, please let us know so we can try to fix it: westernblotanalyzer@gmail.com. Please include the page, what you were trying to do, and the assistive technology or browser you were using.

This statement reflects our current efforts and is not a guarantee of conformance. See also the Privacy Policy and Terms & Disclaimer.
