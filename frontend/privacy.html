Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 5, 2026

The Western Blot Fold Change Analyzer (the “app”) is a free, non-commercial research tool. It is built to work without accounts and without collecting personal information about you. This policy explains, plainly, what the app does and does not do with data.

The short version

  • No accounts, no login, no user database.
  • No analytics, no advertising, no tracking cookies, no fingerprinting.
  • Files you upload are processed temporarily and are not kept.
  • Nothing you do is durably stored — reloading or closing the tab clears your workspace.

What is stored in your browser

The app stores two small values locally in your own browser. Neither is used to identify or track you:

  • A random session ID (in sessionStorage) used only to namespace your temporary uploads for the current tab. It is cleared when the tab closes.
  • A theme preference (light/dark, in localStorage) so the app remembers your display choice.

What happens to files you upload

When you upload blot images (ZIP/TIFF), they are processed to produce your analysis. In the hosted version, images are written as temporary objects and are cleaned up when you delete a blot or close the tab. The app is not a storage product and does not guarantee retention: results, scans, and drawn boxes live only in your current session and are not backed up. Export anything you want to keep before leaving.

Third-party services (sub-processors)

Serving any website necessarily reveals your IP address to the services that deliver it. The app relies on the following third parties, which may receive your IP address as a normal part of handling requests. The app does not control their logging practices, and does not send them your uploaded data for tracking:

  • Vercel — hosting and temporary file storage (Vercel Blob). See Vercel’s privacy policy.
  • Google Fonts — serves the Inter and IBM Plex Mono webfonts.
  • SheetJS CDN — serves the spreadsheet-export library used to build your CSV/XLSX downloads (the export runs in your browser).

Children

This app is intended for researchers and is not directed to children under 13. It does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children.

Your choices

Because the app sets no tracking cookies and keeps no account, there is little to manage. You can clear the app’s stored values at any time by clearing your browser’s site data for this site.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the “Last updated” date above will change. Material changes to how data is handled will be reflected here before they take effect.
